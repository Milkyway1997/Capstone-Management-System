Enter Canvas Access Token:






Don't have a token?
1. Open your Canvas Portal
2. Click on the "Acccount" navigation tab
3. Click Settings
4. Scroll down to the section "Approved Integrations"
5. Click the "New Access Token" button
6. Enter a Purpose and Expiration Date for the Access Token
7. Click "Generate Token"
8. Copy your new Access Token and paste it into the text field above


Need to add more students later?
You can always come back and enter an API Key again to update the database with new students.
